About Us

Welcome to Altos Institute

Welcome to Altos Eduhub, the best software training company in Kochi, Kerala. We are a subsidiary of Altos Technologies, a well-known software development company in Kochi. At Altos Eduhub, we provide comprehensive on-the-job training programs to help graduates gain the skills and experience they need to secure a job in the software industry.

Our training programs cover a range of topics, including Python, React JS, front-end development, digital marketing, and REST API. Our goal is to equip our students with the knowledge and practical experience they need to succeed in today's competitive job market.

As the best software training company in Kochi, we take great pride in our team of experienced instructors who are passionate about teaching and helping students achieve their goals. Our training programs are designed to be hands-on, with a focus on real-world applications and practical experience.

At Altos Eduhub, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible training experience. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology, and our curriculum is constantly updated to reflect the latest industry trends and best practices. We look forward to helping you achieve your career goals and become a part of the thriving software industry.
